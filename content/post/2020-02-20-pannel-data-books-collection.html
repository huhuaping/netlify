  2. Baltagi, B. H. Econometric analysis of panel data [M]. Chichester (West Sussex), John Wiley & Sons, Inc., 2013.

  3. Tsounis, N. and A. Vlachvei. ADVANCES IN PANEL DATA ANALYSIS IN APPLIED ECONOMIC RESEARCH [M], SPRINGER, 2017.

  4. Erik, B. Econometrics of panel data : methods and applications [M], Oxford University Press, 2017.

  5. Croissant, Y. and G. Millo. Panel data econometrics with R [M]. Hoboken, John Wiley & Sons, 2019.
